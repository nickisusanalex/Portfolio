The Residence //

course :

architectural studio 2

The rectangular, south facing, 350 sq.m property, is amidst a residential neighborhood, characterized by its vernacular design. The land topped with red laterite geography, strongly anchors the construction of the G+1 residence. This design focuses in bringing about a space amalgamating the functions and desires of the users while making best use of the site features.
