The Hospital //

course :

architectural studio 6

The erection of this 117-bedded general hospital seeks to revolutionize the healthcare industry of Udupi in providing the city with excellent quality and standards of an institution, as opposed to the local health centers providing just about poor services, along with the absence of specialty clinics.

multi-Specialty

a total of 13 consultation rooms are arranged along the Out Patient Department. The hospital integrates the consultation rooms and the diagnostics center into the same floor and just adjacent to each other, allowing patients an easy and simple one-door system.

The different specialties offered at the hospital are - dental, psychiatrics, orthopedics, gynaecology, opthalmology, dermatology, urology, obstetrics, cardiology, paediatrics and general.
